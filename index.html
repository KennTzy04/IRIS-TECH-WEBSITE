Welcome to Iris Tech

Established in 2024, **Iris Tech** is a beacon of innovation in the realm of technology. Named after the iris of the eye, we envision ourselves as the aperture controlling the amount of light that enters the world of technology, adjusting to the ever-changing landscape of this dynamic industry.

Our journey began with a small team of dedicated engineers and has since grown into a diverse group of experts, each bringing unique skills and perspectives to the table. Despite our recent inception, our commitment to excellence and our passion for problem-solving have propelled us to the forefront of technological advancements.

At Iris Tech, we believe in the power of technology to transform lives and industries. Our mission is to create cutting-edge solutions that address real-world problems, drive progress, and shape the future of technology. We are Iris Tech, illuminating the path to innovation.

Our Story

We, at "Iris Tech", are proud graduates of "Computer Engineering" from "PHINMA Cagayan de Oro College". Our journey began in 2024, when we decided to turn our shared passion for technology and innovation into a reality.

Our academic journey was filled with countless hours of studying, coding, and problem-solving. We tackled complex projects, worked with cutting-edge technologies, and learned from some of the best minds in the field. These experiences not only equipped us with a deep understanding of computer systems and software development but also instilled in us the values of hard work, perseverance, and teamwork.

Upon graduation, we decided to turn our shared passion into a reality and thus, Iris Tech was born. We started in a small office, with nothing more than our laptops and a dream. We worked tirelessly, turning lines of code into innovative solutions that could make a difference in the world.

Over the years, we've faced numerous challenges. There were times when our code didn't work, our designs didn't fit, and our solutions didn't solve. But each challenge was a learning opportunity, a chance to grow, and a step closer to our goals.

Today, Iris Tech has grown into a diverse team of experts, each bringing unique skills and perspectives to the table. We've developed solutions that have transformed industries, created technologies that have improved lives, and built a reputation for excellence and innovation.

But our journey doesn't end here. As we look to the future, we are excited about the possibilities that lie ahead. We are committed to pushing the boundaries of what's possible, to exploring uncharted territories, and to illuminating the path to innovation.

We are Iris Tech, a team of PHINMA Cagayan de Oro College graduates, committed to making a difference in the world of technology. This is our story.
